Heat Wave Resilience Challenge

Heat resilience challenges occur when there is a prolonged period of local excess heat as a sequence of unusually hot days and nights. Heat waves amplify many risks, such as health related or economic risks, including increased human mortality, drought and water quality, wildfire and smoke, power shortages and agricultural losses.

Minor Resilience Challenge

The town normally has great summers, but this summer was a lot hotter than normal, making it hard to enjoy the weather as it was just too hot.
