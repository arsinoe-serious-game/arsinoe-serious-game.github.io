Interventions

The intervention cards for ARSINOE Serious Town are grouped into four resilience types, with each type having seven interventions. Each intervention has positive impacts for the town's resilience, but may also have negative impacts.

Where possible, these interventions have been drawn from the open innovation activities in ARSINOE's Systems Innovation Approach work package. However, we have also added some interventions that were beyond the scope of the open innovation activities budgets and timelines to demonstrate how larger interventions can often have  positive, and negative, effects on a town's resilience.
